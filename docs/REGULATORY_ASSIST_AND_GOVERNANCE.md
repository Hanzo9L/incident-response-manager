# Regulatory Assist and Governance

## Purpose

Support trust and safety teams with faster framework alignment checks while keeping legal and policy decisions human-owned.

## Framework-aware assist flow

1. Incident narrative and metadata are evaluated against framework signal rules.
2. System proposes a framework tag and confidence score.
3. Evidence is surfaced to legal/policy reviewers.
4. Human reviewer accepts, rejects, or requests additional context.
5. Final decision and rationale are logged for audit.

## Example frameworks

- NCMEC-related child safety referral criteria
- EU DSA-style systemic abuse and illegal content patterns

## Non-negotiable guardrails

- no autonomous external reporting
- mandatory reviewer identity and timestamp on referral decisions
- immutable decision log for post-incident and regulatory review
- escalation path required when confidence is low but risk is high

## Metrics for governance

- false-positive rate of framework suggestions
- reviewer override rate
- referral timeliness
- evidence completeness at decision time
- post hoc correction rate
